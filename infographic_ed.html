The Aksharbrahma Satpurush

You cannot build this spaceship alone. The Satpurush is the Master Mechanic who helps you install Dharma, Gnan, Vairagya, and Bhakti.

"Such an Ekantik Dharma is attained only through the association of such an Ekantik Purush."

How to build it?

  • Associate with the Satpurush (Prasang).
  • He gives the strength to imbibe these qualities.
  • He connects you to the command of Shriji Maharaj.
1. Dharma (Righteousness)
The Navigation System & Steering
You are on the highway to Akshardham. Dharma keeps you safe within the lanes. Following the commands (Panch Vartaman, Ahimsa, Brahmacharya) ensures you don't crash off the path.
"No bad thoughts arise in the senses."
SEPARATION OF BODY & ATMA
2. Gnan (Knowledge)
The X-Ray Vision
See the truth. The body is temporary (the outline), but the Atma is eternal (the light). Gnan is realizing you are the Atma (Soul), distinct from the body and Maya.
"Like a sword and its sheath."
3. Vairagya (Detachment)
The Non-Stick Forcefield
The world is full of distractions (sense objects). Vairagya is the shield that makes them bounce off. You interact with the world, but nothing sticks to your atma (soul).
"No desire to enjoy sense objects."

4. Bhakti (Devotion)
The Super Magnet
Feel the pull. Bhakti is intense affection. Your mind and senses should rush towards God like iron filings rushing to a magnet.
"Like a pebble sticking to butter."
Mahatmya (Greatness)
The Infinite Fuel
This is the powerhouse. Understanding God's infinite greatness provides the energy that keeps devotion alive forever.
"With Mahatmya, weak faith becomes strong."
